Getting started using Jounce.

It's quite simple, really. All you need to do is follow the three steps listed below:

1. Choose whether you want to watch a space or nature documentary, and then pick from our selection menu above.

2. If you are looking for space documentaries all information is listed immediately about our current selection.

Note: The selection constantly changes to reflect the current most popular space documentaries. Additionally, documentary series, such as Cosmos, are not included in the selection, only single standalone documentary "movies".

3. If you are looking for nature documentaries you simply need to pick a particular documentary from the selection menu. All documentaries are narrated by David Attenborough, the author's favourite narrator!
